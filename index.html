Who is Aiden?

Hi, I’m Aiden — a 26-year-old multimedia design graduate currently studying web development at EK (formerly KEA). My passion for front-end development started over a decade ago, driven by curiosity and a love for crafting beautiful, intuitive interfaces. Today, I’m channeling that energy into becoming a full-stack developer, expanding my skills in JavaScript, Python, API consumption, and database management.

I’m highly proficient in HTML and CSS, with a growing command of JavaScript and experience in React. My design background gives me a sharp eye for detail and user experience, and I strive to build interfaces that are both visually impressive and seamlessly functional.

I’m especially drawn to projects that empower people — whether that’s through learning platforms, social media tools, or information-rich web apps. My goal is to land an in-house developer role where I can contribute to meaningful products and continue growing alongside a passionate team.
